TrackAlert App

Personal Safety & Tracker Detection Alerts

TrackAlert App helps users stay safe by detecting nearby tracking devices and sending alerts when suspicious tracking behavior is identified. This provides users with awareness of potential stalking or unwanted following.

How It Works

The app monitors for nearby tracking signals and identifies patterns where a device may be moving with a user over time. When such behavior is detected, the app notifies the user so they can take appropriate action.

Features

  • Real-time tracker detection alerts
  • Background monitoring for suspicious movement
  • User-controlled alert settings
  • Privacy-focused design

Privacy

TrackAlert does not sell or share user phone numbers for marketing purposes. Data is only used to provide safety-related functionality.

Contact

SMS Consent & Messaging Policy (Twilio Compliance)

This section describes how users consent to receive SMS messages and how messaging is handled in compliance with applicable regulations.

Opt-In Process

Users opt in to receive SMS messages directly within the TrackAlert App by entering their phone number and explicitly agreeing to receive notifications. Consent is collected through a clear affirmative action such as a checkbox or form submission.

Types of Messages

  • Tracker detection alerts
  • Safety notifications regarding potential stalking or tracking
  • Important service-related updates

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies and depends on tracker detection events. Messages are only sent when relevant safety events occur.

Opt-Out

Users can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, no further SMS messages will be sent unless the user opts in again.

Help

Users can reply HELP for assistance.

Fees

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on the user’s mobile carrier.

Compliance

TrackAlert App sends messages only to users who have provided explicit consent. Messages are limited to safety-related and service-related purposes only.

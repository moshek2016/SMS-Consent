SMS Consent & Messaging Policy

Last updated: April 2026

About Our Service

TrackAlert App provides safety notifications to users, including alerts related to potential tracker detection to help prevent stalking or unwanted following.

How Users Provide Consent

Users provide consent to receive SMS messages by opting in directly within the TrackAlert App. This includes explicitly agreeing to receive messages, such as by selecting a checkbox or submitting a form within the app.

Types of Messages

  • Tracker detection alerts
  • Safety notifications related to potential stalking or following

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on detected tracker activity and user interaction within the app.

Opt-Out Instructions

Users can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message. Once opted out, no further messages will be sent unless the user opts in again.

Help Instructions

For assistance, users can reply HELP to any message.

Fees

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier.

Privacy

We respect user privacy and do not share phone numbers with third parties for marketing purposes.

Contact Information

For support or questions, please contact us through the TrackAlert App.

SMS Consent & Messaging Policy

Last updated: April 2026

About Our Service

This application provides notifications and updates to users who opt in to receive SMS messages.

How Users Provide Consent

Users provide consent to receive SMS messages by opting in directly within our application. This occurs through an explicit action such as selecting a checkbox or submitting a form indicating agreement to receive messages.

Types of Messages

  • Order updates and notifications
  • Account-related alerts
  • Service-related messages

Message Frequency

Message frequency may vary depending on user activity but typically does not exceed a few messages per week.

Opt-Out Instructions

Users can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message. After opting out, users will no longer receive messages unless they opt in again.

Help Instructions

For assistance, users can reply HELP or contact support.

Fees

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier.

Privacy

We respect user privacy and do not share phone numbers with third parties for marketing purposes.
